Re: MAOIFrameBuffer iOS crash

by JDance » Thu Jan 16, 2014 6:52 am

The stack trace seems to say that when MOAI tries to retrieve an entry from your render table (by integer index) you get a crash in the lua runtime. I guess basically your rendertable is corrupt or gets corrupted during the render loop. Where and when do you modify your render table? Put all such modifications through some functions so you can log where it happens. And make sure the render table is a pure array-style table so that lua_rawgeti will work.

Disclaimer: I'm not really sure what I'm talking about but it's something at least
