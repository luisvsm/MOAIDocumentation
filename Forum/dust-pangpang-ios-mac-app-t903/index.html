Dust PangPang: iOS/Mac App

by refactor » Tue Jun 19, 2012 11:31 am

Our team published Moai-based game Dust PangPang to the iTunes App Store. Dust PangPang is a physics based action game to fight with dusts.

‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥
Once upon a time in a peaceful dollhouse, a bunch of Dust invaded the dollhouse and were making a mess. She decided to fight Dust and is about to clean them away.
★ Retina Display-Ready for iPhone 4 and the new iPad / No iPhone 3Gs Support ★
‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥
● 4 gorgeously crafted dollhouses with 80 stages. Each dollhouse is made up of its own unique fixtures.
● Upgradable weapons: double shots, hook shots, shooting stars that can clean all dust.
● Life saving items: bonus life, shield
● Survival mode: prepare for surviving from continuous dust invasion.
‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥‥

We build higher level framework on top of Moai and used TexturePacker and Tiled to make this game.

We aim to publish this game to Mac App Store later this week. And our next game is plan to release this week on iTunes App Store and Android Play Market.

Thanks to Moai.

- Sung Kim.
