Asterox (Android)

Posted: Sun Apr 19, 2015 2:18 am
by Motig
Finally released a game, big thanks to everyone on the forums and irc for all the help and contributions to moai!

The game is called Asterox and it's about collecting... asteroids. It's a challenging arcade game with the main goal being beating yours and other people their highscores. There's online leaderboards although I reset them for release as I didn't want my own personal scores to clutter it!

Check it out at: https://play.google.com/store/apps/deta ... ft.asterox

If you have any thoughts about what you dislike/like, would love to hear them for a next game. There's some things I wanted to change but at some point I had to call it a day and finish what I had. I've started many game projects but finishing one is definitely harder than expected!
