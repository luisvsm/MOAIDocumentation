Re: how do you choose which device to support ?

by Vavius » Sun Mar 29, 2015 3:26 pm

Can you provide some examples of bugs you encounter?

Our games are targeting all armeabi-v7a devices. Works fine on all devices that we have + I don't see bad reviews caused by device incompatibilities.

Some bugs I've faced when porting to android:
1) Fonts/shaders lost when suspending/resuming the app
2) Back button support causes much headache (another way to trigger scene transitions when you don't expect)
3) Different screen sizes. Currently we support 3 aspect ratios (16:9, 4:3, 3:2), choosing best and scaling to fit
4) Shader syntax can differ. (Example, you can't use "smooth" as variable name on some GPUs)
