Hah no you're right, it's not required for MOAI, I just wasn't thinking correctly. The RUBE loader can do the conversion.

Once I've got a decent RUBE to MOAI JSON loader going I'll publish it here, and possibly contribute it as a sample in the 1.5-dev branch too.

RUBE isn't perfect, but it's a very handy tool that is pretty cheap. It was some crashing issues, but you can work around them fairly easily. It's certainly a heck of a lot easier creating Box2D scenes and importing them into MOAI than it is than actually trying to hand roll a MOAI Box2D editor, or even worse...hand roll Box2D scenes in MOAI Lua code.
