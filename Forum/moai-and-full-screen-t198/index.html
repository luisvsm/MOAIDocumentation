MOAI and full-screen

by patrick » Thu Nov 17, 2011 12:27 pm

Yes, EVENT_STOP or EVENT_TIMER_LOOP are the right ones to look at. EVENT_TIMER_KEYFRAME is a little ad hoc... you can attach a curve to a timer and use EVENT_TIMER_KEYFRAME to get a callback whenever a keyframe is encountered. So You can use it to do things like time sound effects with animation. Like I said, a little ad hoc.

I will look into full screen mode on glut. Will add a demo for that before next release.

Re: MOAI and full-screen

by ibisum » Mon Jun 18, 2012 9:16 am

You will have to address full-screen mode in the *HOST* sources, not in any MOAI sources. The host sources are configured to get a GL View window set up and instantiated within the context of the outer 'container' for each platform - for Android, its based on the Layout config, and for iOS its based on the bundle settings for the project. If you want full-screen with no menu-bar, you have to tell both iOS and Android to do it, in the host, before it will make any sense in the MOAI environment.
