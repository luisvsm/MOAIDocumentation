Android and iOS Features

by patrick » Tue Sep 13, 2011 6:28 pm

Hey there. I should revisit that doc... abandon is too strong a word. We aren't actively supporting the FMOD extension, but we know that people are using it.

Untz is still very new, so I'll let Robert chime in on the stage of its development for Android. We've been using it internally on Windows and iPhone, but haven't put it through its paces on Android yet.

No feature grid, but (off the top of my head) you can expect parity across the majority of the framework. Other than sound, all of the animation, graphics, physics and utility objects should be supported.

Android and iOS Features

by patrick » Fri Sep 16, 2011 12:00 am

If I were in your shoes I'd check out untz first. If it doesn't meet your needs, post here. I'll follow up with Robert; he should be able to fix it for you. If you don't have time for that (i.e. you're shipping soon and needs sound right away), FMOD is basically the industry standard and has (I feel) a very reasonable license for casual/mobile products. You can also always use FMOD for one platform, then switch over to untz when it is more mature.

Of course, personally I hope you'll try untz and help improve it by reporting your experiences, even if you don't wind up using it.
