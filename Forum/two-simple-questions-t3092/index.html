I had tried with gdb already, but i needed to recompile/relink with -gp and i had no breakpoints either, so i could just "run" & "exec", and I basically had same as just straight executing.

Here's how I fixed it, maybe useful for someone in the future

I ltraced moai, and I saw missing OpenGL support in SDL, so I installed SDL-OpenGL libraries(and headers) and I manually set VIDEO_OPENGL:BOOL=ON in build/build-linux/CMakeCache.txt and ran bin/build-all-linux.sh again ... SDLOpenGL was built, and now I can run the samples fine !

Still have an error with creating the Android-host though, but I'll figure that out later
