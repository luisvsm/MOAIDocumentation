Sorry just have time to post the lighting links, I will do a full write up sometime in the future.

The theory was have a fast dynamic light system based on math not shaders.
1. query all physics objects with in range of the light
2. Draw a light as triangle-fan to an FBO with DEPTHMASK and additive blending, based on norms.
3. clip shadows out of the light before drawing, ctDifference, clipper --> http://www.angusj.com/delphi/clipper.php
4. Draw the FBO to screen with multiply blending.


Results: right-click view as
