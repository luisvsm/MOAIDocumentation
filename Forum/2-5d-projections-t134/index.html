by patrick » Thu Sep 29, 2011 12:45 pm

That is pretty nifty. It won't be super performant, but you could use a custom mesh (MOAIMesh) to build your page animation each frame. Do all the math in Lua then convert your points to vertices for rendering.

At some point we'll add support for some built-in geometry effects like this, but could be a while before we get to it.
