Sound support, FMOD, FMOD Studio, ...

by JKON » Wed Nov 26, 2014 8:59 pm

Hi..!

I've been trying to find a good sound platform for moai, as the "standard" Untz doesn't support playing with different sample rates/pitches. That feature is critical as we need to make engine sounds.

I tried FMOD Studio (https://github.com/Vavius/moai-fmod-studio) plugin already and got it up and running on mingw/glut and android. The problem is that for some reason the android build crashes on start on older/lower budget devices. This is bad as the game itself runs smoothly around 60fps on even the low end device.

Devices tested so far with FMOD Studio:

- Samsung ACE3 crashes on start
- Sony XPeria Z works fine
- Samsung Galaxy Tab 4 works fine
- Nexus 5 works fine

Long story short(er): What is the recommended sound engine that we can use for low end devices as well? I believe in the emerging markets, low end devices are still very popular and we would not like to set the requirements for the device higher just because the sounds are not working. One option would be to build a custom up/downsample engine on top on Untz using sample buffers, but I believe it is going to affect the overall game performance.

Best Regards,

Re: Sound support, FMOD, FMOD Studio, ...

by JKON » Fri Nov 28, 2014 6:50 am

Found a solution with FMOD STUDIO:

The problem was that I was trying to load libmoai.so first, then libfmod.so in MoaiActivity.java. That was working fine with 3 of the 4 devices tested. When changed to libfmod.so first then libmoai.so, it works also with ACE3!
