by naturally » Thu Mar 26, 2015 9:35 pm

Time to collapse in a pile of exhaustion!! Thanks for the well wishes! It is fantastic seeing as how everyone (myself included) wrote the whole venture off as a failure halfway through. But I never gave up trying, even at that last minute.

I'll now relinquish my spam-hold on this website and give you all more room to breathe and let the rest of your great games here shine! If anyone needs help with their projects done the road I will be glad to lend you my support. I'll also be contributing funds back to MOAI, in the form of contracting some (hopefully small) feature requests that go back onto github. It isn't a ton of money (was supposed to kick in at 10% of 50k), and I'll give Patrick first dibs, but if for whatever reason that doesn't work out (he's always been too super busy to take on extra contracts) I'll ask here. I can't just give money as a donation as initially planned because it's against the Kickstarter rules.

There are more details about this stuff here: Tools Support Update and here: Development Update #10.
