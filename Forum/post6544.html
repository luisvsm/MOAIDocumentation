MOAI Sessions @ Metalab, Vienna (Austria)

by ibisum » Thu Sep 13, 2012 11:15 am

Hi!

We're starting our first MOAI workshops at Metalab tomorrow:

https://metalab.at/wiki/Moai

Metalab is a world-famous hackerspace here in Vienna, Austria, well known for being a center for lots of new, wonderful technology, and as an open public space for creative hacking, all interested parties are invited to participate, learn, and most importantly: MAKE!

I'm going to start off the sessions tomorrow with an introduction to MOAI basics, the SDK, and maybe promote some of the existing MOAI releases that are out there, and hopefully by the end of it we'll feel motivated to have a MOAI code jam too, with maybe some new people introduced this wonderful technology.

If you're in the neighborhood, drop by and join us! Its free to the public, informal, and we will have a lot of fun!
