Re: Skyturns released!

by JDance » Mon Nov 17, 2014 1:38 pm

Thanks for trying everyone!

Vavius wrote:Cool, took W66436KRPY4X
Nice game.

What approach did you use for outline shader? Expanding backfaces along normals?


It was more tricky than I wanted it to be. I ended up expanding the whole polygon by computing a vector from the center point of each "corner" to every vertex and then multiplying it to make it longer. Effectively thickening the structure of the level. Then I draw the thicker level mesh in black with a modified depth buffer z to get it to draw just behind the level poly! I also make the thickening factor larger further away to make it seem more like a constant thickness outline. Otherwise it would become invisibly thin further away.

Expanding backfaces was my first approach but it would give artifacts in corners where the faces would kinda separate!
