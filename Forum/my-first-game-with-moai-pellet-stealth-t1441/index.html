Re: My first game with Moai : Pellet Stealth

by bartoleo » Wed Dec 12, 2012 9:44 am

look at levels (ex. http://github.com/bartoleo/pellet-moai/ ... vel001.lua)
levels are very very very very simple
# are 'roof' of walls
* are walls (| is a column ... just different tiles)
@ is player

0-9,a-b,A-B are placeholders positions

enemies:
_level.enemies={
gui_A={pos="A",char=4,actions={"lookAround"}}
}
objects (chests,keys,torchs,doors...)
_level.objects={
}

and you can add custom 'logic' to enterlevel (intro of level)
or level

--- enterlevel custom ---------------------------------------------------------------------
--_level.enterLevelWait = 2
--funciton _level.enterlevelLoad(self,layer) end
--function _level.enterLevelUpdate(self) end
--function _level.enterLevelUnload(self) end
--- level custom callbacks -------------------------------------------------------------------
--function _level.update(self) end
--fucntion _level.unload (self) end
