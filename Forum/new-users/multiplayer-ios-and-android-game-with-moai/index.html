Welcome to Moai, thanks for checking us out.

If I understand what you are asking, then the answer is yes you can do that with Moai and Moai Cloud.  Within Moai Cloud you would have to develop a couple of restful services (written in Lua) that handle storing and retrieving your data.  Then on the client side you can use luacurl within your app to access your service on the cloud.

 

Hopefully that is enough to get you started, if you need more detailed answers just let me know.
