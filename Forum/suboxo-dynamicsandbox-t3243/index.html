So awesome that you mention Lolo, that was totally an inspiration for us when designing the game! The name is actually a sort of amalgamation of Lolo and Boxxle (which is the American name for Sokoban). We started with box puzzles as the base, and developed new game mechanics around that to spice things up. We're going to have some better screens soon (animated gifs, etc.). Can't wait to get the game out into the world. We'll also be at GDC in the March - if anyone is attending, come by our booth.
