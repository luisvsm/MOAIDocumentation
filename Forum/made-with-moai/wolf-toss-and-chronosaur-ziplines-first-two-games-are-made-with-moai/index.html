Zipline is shipping two great games in the months to come, and of course they are Made with Moai!

Wolf Toss

This is the game we demonstrated at GDC.  Guide the Big Bad Wolf in his battle against the Three Little Pigs in Zipline’s challenging new physics puzzler Wolf Toss. The gameplay is the familiar physics puzzle style made popular by Crush the Castle and Angry Birds, with a retro animation look. Wolf Toss is free to play on iPhone and Android, and you can earn points in-game to add keen Wolf power-ups and challenging new levels.

 

Even better news – we'll be sharing the Wolf Toss source with Moai developers as an example app.

 

Chronosaur

Our second game is Chronosaur, ﻿an arcade action game. Chronosaur has escaped the shackles of linear time progression; now he must fight against those who would send him back. Guide him as he rampages through history, devouring all who dare stand against him. How much will you annihilate in your quest to get to the next level?

 

You can read the developer diaries for insight on the development of each game.
