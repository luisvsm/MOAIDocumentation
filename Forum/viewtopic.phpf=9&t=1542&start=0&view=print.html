Been on Corona SDK for the last 11 months and now looking at Moai.

I have 3 apps on the iOS App Store (well 2 and 1 in review).

Just wondering the best way to get started really, I have Moai installed and working fine on my MacBook. So want to jump in and start developing my first game/app with it.

In case anyone is wondering why am looking elsewhere -

Firstly money (or lack of it) and not being able afford to renew my Corona license. Also I want to get my apps on Android, Kindle HD and Nook and with Corona you need to buy the professional license for that.

Secondly Moai is open source and hopefully will get stuff developed for it by other teams. For instance I know a few guys over on Hacker News are discussing getting Ouya support working. Also am sure DoubleFine will find things they want to improve or add new stuff. This sort of thing is just not possible with Corona (unless they buy your company ).
