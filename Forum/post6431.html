I am working on a series of tutorials for users getting started with Moai. Each one goes into a substantial amount of detail and should be accessible for new developers, and hopefully useful for experienced developers checking out Moai. They are written in a conversational style.

As of right now, there are 3 core tutorials, and a couple "guides".

Part One: Opening a Window and creating a viewport

Part Two: Loading and displaying a sprite

Part Three: Touching and clicking


Guides:
Configuring IntelliJ to work with Moai

Building a Moai Android application

Installation/Hello World


And a Table of Contents link.


I hope they prove useful. I have many more in the works. If you spot any errors, have any suggestions or comments, please fire away!
