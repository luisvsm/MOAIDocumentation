What I am working on...

by patrick » Thu Jul 14, 2011 9:23 pm

Just a quick update... all of our resources have been on pushing out open beta and supporting partner developers in the last few weeks. There are some key partner projects in the final stages of production that have commanded our attention.

I am back on OpenGL ES 2.0 support this sprint and will get to the addition of a virtual file system soon after. Our partner studios need it, we need it, the community needs it... it's a no brainer. There are also some incremental improvements to the hosts, but I'm more excited about what Bach (and other volunteers) are doing.

With the support effort we've sort of wound up in an 'always a bridesmaid' type situation with our own game projects. The good news is we just added more staff - some fantastic devs, scripters and artists - so will be able to accelerate both Wolf Toss and Chronosaur. There are also some new cloud features in the works that I'm personally super excited about.

Thanks to the growing community of participants so far. The questions, comments and contributions are a huge help. I wish we had more time to do really extensive documentation, but in the mean time any and all questions continue to be welcome in the forum. At some point in the near future there will be some changes to the community web site, including a wiki. If anyone wants to volunteer on the documentation side, please let me know.
