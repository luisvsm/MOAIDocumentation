Razy Raccoon

by btatarov » Sun Mar 16, 2014 4:13 pm

I needed to test a new graphical designer and since everybody is doing flappy bird clones these days, I decided why not. The whole project was done in just a few days.

It uses heavily modified MOAI 1.2 (Build 56), multiple advertising networks with an ad rotator (Chartboost, Playhaven, AdMob, Heyzap, StartApp and RevMob) and multiple leaderboard solutions (Google Play Game Services, Amazon Game Circle and the usual iOS Game Center). I may write a blog post of the modifications I did and why I decided to downgrade to 1.2 at some point.

by btatarov » Thu Mar 20, 2014 4:28 pm

@scottwb, I think 0.30 is too little for US and UK. I leave most of the world at $0.20, UK at $0.35 and US ad $0.40 but I think if you want to target mainly these countries then you need to at least do $0.40 for UK and $0.45 for US and leave the rest out. The counties that deliver most clicks for $0.20 in my situation are Brasil, Turkey, Colombia, India, Ukraine, India, Philippines and Thailand. It's up to you if you need these.

P.S. Can you tell me how you set your Facebook ad campaigns? I once tried that but was receiving something like $0.18-0.20 CPC which is much much higher than $0.30-40 CPI from App Brain for US. I stopped the campaign after $60.
