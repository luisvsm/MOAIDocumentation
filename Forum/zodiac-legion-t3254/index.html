Zodiac Legion
My game is far from being finished (I need to add sound, AI, and lots of content), but I thought I should as well put it there, because it will still be quite a long time before it is.
It is a tactics game, with a touch of strategy and RPG, in which you lead small parties of heroes and retainers to reclaim the craddle of the Empire after it has been overun by barbarians and monsters.
It will be available on Steam (I have not decided on which platforms yet).
It draws heavily from X-COM and Descent : Road to Legend (aka modern Heroquest).

I am porting it to moai 1.7, then I will resume adding content.
