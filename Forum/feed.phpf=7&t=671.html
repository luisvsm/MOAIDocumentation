Your virtual currency doesn't need to radically change the game play. A lot of the items we sell in Wolf Toss are decorative, but everyone likes customizing their Wolf and buying fx trails etc. Even a small amount of revenue from virtual goods is likely to be better than banner ads. Also, our Tapjoy integration is currently targeted at the offer wall piece, not the banners.

Another idea would be to do something like AdColony video ads at the beginning of a level, and give players the option to buy an unlock to remove the ads. We try not to do that where possible because it can be quite annoying.

We've looked at a couple of others. For example, we are experimenting with AdColony in Wolf Toss for incentivized video from an interstitial.

Mobile banner type ads aren't going to do anything for your revenue because the eCPM is low and users just don't click on them. You would need millions and millions of users to make any substantial revenue from traditional banner ads. Much better to build a freemium game and tie ads/offers to a virtual currency reward using Tapjoy or AdColony.

Let me know any other questions.
