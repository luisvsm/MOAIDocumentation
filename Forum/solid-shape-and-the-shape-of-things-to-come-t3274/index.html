[Ludum Dare 35] Solid Shape, and the shape of things to come

by MaxTeel » Mon Apr 18, 2016 8:41 pm

I participated in last weekend's Ludum Dare (http://ludumdare.com/compo/) on the 48 hour category.

The theme was "Shapeshift", and I ended up making a puzzle game with a very generous inspiration on a well known stealth game series

http://ludumdare.com/compo/ludum-dare-3 ... &uid=11314

I've been casually following MOAI for a long time, but haven't done anything big with with. With this project, I realized that I am really comfortable with it to tackle a larger project.
The architecture is mostly sound. The scene manager is something I have been developing slowly over the year (inspired by Zipline's one). But on the last half of Sunday I just forgot good practices for just "getting things done"

I big, big thank you for all of the community. Special thanks to everyone involved in Pito and moaiforge.com. To thrive, MOAI has to be easier to use for newcomers. Having updated binaries and documentation is a must. So, thank you. A lot!
