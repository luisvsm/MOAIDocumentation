Re: what is the fix for untz delay in windows?

by ezraanderson » Sun Mar 17, 2013 11:00 pm

i use Audacity (audacity.sourceforge.net) and export *.ogg files at 96000hz (bottom left corner in audacity)

then initialize untz in lua with

Code: Select all
  1.  

  2. MOAIUntzSystem.initialize (96000, 8192)

  3.  




Its most definitely, is an over kill for sample rates, but for some reason it works. You will probably still notice a small delay but most other people won't notice the delay.


For ios I do everything the same but use 22050hz

Code: Select all
  1.  

  2.  

  3.         MOAIUntzSystem.initialize (22050, 8192)

  4.  



(spec)
