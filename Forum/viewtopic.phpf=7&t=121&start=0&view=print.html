Right now, yes and no. The main list of layers held by the Sim is a stack, but there's some little used functionality that lets you nest layers inside of other layers as props. In theory, you could use a layer's priority sorting to sort the other layers it contains. I haven't tried that, but if it's important to you let me know. I've been meaning to go back for another look at nested layer support but have been a bit swamped this week and last.
