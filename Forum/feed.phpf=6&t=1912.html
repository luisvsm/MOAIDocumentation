2. Put a grid in side the FBO, bind a light texture and set the tiles to draw overlapped
Code:
  1.  

  2. map_grid['fogs_inside'] = MOAIGrid.new ()

  3. map_grid['fogs_inside']:setSize ( map['width']*2,map['height']*2, 16, 16,0,0,16,16 )

  4. map_grid['fogs_inside']:initRectGrid( map['width']*2,map['height']*2,16,16,-100,-100) --x2 X4  

  5.  


3. the eight tiles closes to the hero get enabled by
Code:
  1.  

  2. :setTile(fogX, fogY, 1) -- 1 is the light texture or disable it if you want black

  3.  


Code:
  1.  

  2. local fX       = (math.floor(heroX/16))

  3. local fY       = (math.floor(heroY/16))

  4. local fogX=fX+1-5

  5. local fogY=fY+1-3

  6.       for y = 1, 4 do    

  7.        for x = 1,4 do    

  8.  

  9.              map_grid['fogs_inside']:setTile(fogX, fogY, 1)            

  10.          

  11.           fogX = fogX + 1      

  12.        end

  13.       fogX =fX+1-2

  14.       fogY =fogY + 1  

  15.     end

  16.
