Hello, i start to make a little sidescroller and now i have the following Problem:
The resolution of my background graphic is very low (pixelart).
When i move the background (bg:setLoc()) to simulate a walking character, the background graphic is not painted at once. Means there are some strange effekts while moving. For example the upper part of the graphics are a pixel in front of the rest background.
I think the reason is that moai tries to paint the image while it's not ready in the video ram!?
Is there a way to avoid this?

Hopefully my explanation is understandable.
Greetings,
oop

EDIT:
Okay, i figured it out by myself.
Moving the camera + character instead of moving the background works fine.
But anyway it's a little bit strange because the result should be same, right!?
