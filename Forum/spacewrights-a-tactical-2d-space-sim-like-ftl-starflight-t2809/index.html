Spacewrights: A tactical 2D space sim like FTL + Starflight

by Drake250 » Wed Sep 17, 2014 10:43 pm

http://spacewrights.com/

Spacewrights is a tactical sci-fi 2D spaceship simulation game where you explore, build, and create a galactic empire for PC, Mac, Linux, Android & iOS platforms. We released a demo today alongside our Kickstarter and Steam Greenlight campaigns.

  • Demo (custom windows 1.5 SDL host): http://spacewrights.com/demo
  • Kickstarter: http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/arithmebit/spacewrights
  • Steam Greenlight: http://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=306657784

Pics:












Moai stuff:
I'm the lead developer and started writing Spacewrights last year using the Moai 1.4 Glut and Android hosts. These forums were incredibly useful to get off the ground. Since then I've continued plugging away and recruiting help to build this demo. We eventually moved on to 1.5. The Windows SDL is our primary host along with Linux for development. We're also using a heavily modified and extended version of Hanappe's excellent flower library for gui and display elements. Overall Moai has been a joy to use to get this demo up an running. There's a lot of fantastic knowledge here on the forums. I'd like to give a personal shout out to ezraanderson, ibisum, nosheet, thebullno1, JDance, BacioiuC, naturally, and everyone else on the forums for helping me on my topics/private messages, and just generally having excellent posts that help overcome the documentation issues Moai has.

Assuming Spacewrights gets funded I hope to open source more of my Moai changes, especially all the flower enhancements. All our additions to the SDL host (mostly resolution/refresh rate handling) are available on github (https://github.com/ArithmeBit/moai-dev). It's a little behind moai master because we focused on demo bug fixed, but we plan to bringing it back up to date.

I'd be happy to answer any questions about Spacewrights, or how it has been developed on Moai so far.

Despite my game and FTL appearing similar, they are pretty different. FTL is at its core an excellent roguelike. My game is story and exploration focused. You maintain a game save and continue to import your fleet and crew, no restarts when you die. It's really more similar to Starflight than FTL, but few people remember that game.
